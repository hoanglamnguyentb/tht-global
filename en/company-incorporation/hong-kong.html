1. Can a foreigner register a company in Hong Kong ?

Foreign individuals/companies have the opportunity to register a company in Hong Kong with full ownership rights. Under the Hong Kong Companies Act, both local residents and foreigners can legally own 100% of a Hong Kong company.

2. What kinds of business are allowed in Hong Kong?

Hong Kong presents a multitude of business prospects and warmly embraces diverse industries. It provides favorable conditions for sectors such as e-commerce, healthcare, tourism, IT, trading, finance, and more. Explore the extensive opportunities Hong Kong has to offer for your business with us.

3. What are the initial requirements for incorporating a company in Hong Kong?

Requirements for establishing a company in Hong Kong include:

  • A minimum of one director and one shareholder, who can be the same individual
  • A local registered address in Hong Kong for your company
  • A local company secretary
